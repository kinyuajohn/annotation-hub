Fotress Assembly | City of Refuge

Fortress Assembly is a contemporary Christian church that believes in Jesus, a church that loves God and people. We are excited about the gift of salvation we have found in Jesus are passionate about seeing God’s kingdom established across the earth. We are a prayer ministry that brings together men and women who want to grow in effective and fervent prayer to have much power made available to them.
